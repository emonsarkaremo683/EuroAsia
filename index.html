About Euro-Asia

Built on Craftsmanship,
Driven by Quality

Founded in 2020 in Bangladesh, Euro-Asia Apparels Ltd is a 100% export oriented knit Garments company. We specialize in woven and knit apparel for men, women, and children — serving buyers from the USA, EU, UK, Japan, and Australia.

Our 80,000 sq. ft. facility integrates cutting-edge automation with skilled craftsmanship to meet the most demanding global compliance standards.
