About EUROASIA

Built on Craftsmanship,
Driven by Quality

Founded in 2004 in Dhaka, Bangladesh, EUROASIA Co. has grown into one of the region's most trusted garment manufacturers. We specialize in woven and knit apparel for men, women, and children — serving buyers from the USA, EU, UK, Japan, and Australia.

Our 180,000 sq. ft. facility integrates cutting-edge automation with skilled craftsmanship to meet the most demanding global compliance standards.
